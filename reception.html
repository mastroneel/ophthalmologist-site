The doctor receives patients on the 3rd floor of the De Lange House, at Pikk Street 11, in the Pärnu downtown near the bus station. There is a billboard “Silmaarst Elle Lepik" (Ophthalmologist Elle Lepik) next to the door. The building is situated just across the street from Port Artur I.
